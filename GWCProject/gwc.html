Hello, I'm Kylah Brooke Covey, the one behind this webpage. At 18, I'm a senior at Estill County High School, dabbling in computer science classes at the Estill County Area Technology Center for three years now, where I enjoy coding.

I've snagged certifications like IC3 G6, HTML/CSS, and I'm gearing up for JavaScript. Besides academics, I keep busy with various activities like Varsity Softball, Volleyball, and Cheer teams, along with roles in FBLA, TSA, STLP, Student Government, Beta Club, and SkillsUSA. I've had success in web design competitions, winning at regional, state (1st place), and national (14th place) levels, with a 2nd place finish in the previous state competition.

I built this webpage from scratch, starting with just a blank page on the computer. The creation of this webpage is for the Girls Who Code STLP Competition, which also stands as a platform to share knowledge about AI and its benefits.
